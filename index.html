    PhytoCare

    A hybrid approach for identifying Rice, Potato and Corn diseases

    Read More

    Agriculture plays a vital role in Bangladeshâ€™s economy. It is important to ensure proper growth and health of crops for the agricultural sector. Crop diseases are a potential threat to agricultural productivity and food security in Bangladesh and a timely and accurate identification of crop diseases is critical for sustaining food production. This study presents deep learning methods, and support vector machines (SVM) to predict crop diseases. Our proposed model is designed for classifying diseases of three crops, Rice, Potato and Corn. A hybrid model has been implemented for enhanced performance and accuracy by employing EfficientNetB0 for feature extraction and SVM for classification. EfficientNetB0 is a Convolutional Neural Network (CNN) model renowned for its ability to achieve high learning rates swiftly. SVM is a classical machine learning algorithm renowned for its proficiency in classification tasks. This hybrid model addresses the challenges and computational efficiency often encountered in precision agriculture applications. The proposed hybrid model achieved 97.29% accuracy.
