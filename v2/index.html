  • 01

    PhytoCare

    A hybrid approach for identifying Rice, Potato and Corn diseases

    Read More

    Agriculture plays a vital role in Bangladesh’s economy. It is important to ensureproper growth and health of crops for the agricultural sector. Crop diseases are apotential threat to agricultural productivity and food security in Bangladesh and atimely and accurate identification of crop diseases is critical for sustaining food pro-duction. This study presents deep learning methods, and support vector machines(SVM) to predict crop diseases. Our proposed model is designed for classifying dis-eases of three crops, Rice, Potato and Corn. A hybrid model has been implementedfor enhanced performance and accuracy by employing EfficientNetB0 for featureextraction and SVM for classification. EfficientNetB0 is a Convolutional Neural Net-work (CNN) model renowned for its ability to achieve high learning rates swiftly.SVM is a classical machine learning algorithm renowned for its proficiency in clas-sification tasks. This hybrid model addresses the challenges and computationalefficiency often encountered in precision agriculture applications.The proposedhybrid model achieved 97.29% accuracy. A comparative analysis with other mod-els, CNN, VGG16, ResNet50, Xception, Mobilenet V2, Autoencoders, Inceptionv3 and Efficient B0 each achieving an accuracy of 86.57%, 83.29%, 68.79%, 94.07%, 90.71%, 87.90%, 94.14% , 96.14% respectively. Our proposed model achieved abetter accuracy than the other models. (PDF) PhytoCare : A hybrid approach for identifying Rice, Potato and Corn diseases. Available from: https://www.researchgate.net/publication/378395574_PhytoCare_A_hybrid_approach_for_identifying_Rice_Potato_and_Corn_diseases#fullTextFileContent [accessed Mar 08 2024].
